  • Simplified Code: Reduced from ~1400 lines to ~400 lines (70% reduction)

  • Automatic Renderer Selection: Chooses MN2MNRenderer or ST2MNRenderer based on content
  • Modern ES6 Modules: Clean imports and exports with proper dependency management
